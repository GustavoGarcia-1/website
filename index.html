About Me

Hi, I'm Gustavo — an Electrical Engineering student with a passion for robotics, autonomous systems, and intelligent hardware design. I'm drawn to the challenge of making machines that can perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.

My work spans embedded systems, control theory, and machine learning for robotics — from motor controllers and custom PCBs to locomotion research and manipulation.

I'm actively looking for research and industry opportunities in the robotics space. Feel free to reach out.
