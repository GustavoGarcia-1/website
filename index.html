About Me

Hi, I'm Gustavo an Electrical Engineering engineering undergraduate with a passion for robotics, autonomous systems, and intelligent hardware design. I'm drawn to the challenge of making machines that can perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.

My work spans machine learning for robotics, through vision language action models, reinforcement learning, and more.

In my free time I enjoy staying active through sports like basketball, football, and tennis. I also love reading about the development of jazz music, during the era of people like Louis Armstrong and Malcom X.

I'm actively looking for research and industry opportunities in the robotics space
