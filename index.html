Projects

Foundation Models · LeRobot

Coffee Making Robot

Collected demonstration dataset via teleoperation on so101 arms with multiple camera angles, covering the full coffee making sequence. Trained π0.5 using LeRobot, experimenting with quantile and mean std normalization to improve action prediction quality.
Foundation Models · LeRobot

Cube Stacking Robot

Trained smolVLA, ACT, and π0.5 on so101 robotic arms with hours of teleoperation data to finetune and compare models for cube stacking. Ran training on H100 GPUs through VESSL AI, Google Colab, and SDSC, uploading checkpoints to HuggingFace for reproducibility.
RL · Mujoco · PPO

Pick and Place Robot

Built on a GPU accelerated RL environment for a robot arm in MuJoCo MJX and Brax, running 2048 parallelized rollouts in JAX. Designed multi phase shaped rewards with tanh distance shaping and velocity based stillness penalties. Trained PPO to 160M+ environment steps, tracked via Weights & Biases.
RL · MJLab · PPO

This project is part of an emerging research fighting league for robots under Engine AI, and we are currently participating in the prelimary rounds. We were tasked to imitate human fighting motions to the best of our abilities with the given dataset.
